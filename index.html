🌍 About FIFA World Cup 2026

Everything you need to know about the biggest sporting event on the planet

🏟️

16 Host Cities

Spread across the USA, Canada, and Mexico — making this the first World Cup co-hosted by three nations simultaneously.

🌐

48 Teams

Expanded from 32 to 48 nations, giving more countries than ever the chance to compete on football's greatest stage.

⚽

104 Matches

The most matches ever played in a single World Cup — over five weeks of non-stop football action from group stage to final.

📅

June – July 2026

The tournament runs from mid-June through mid-July 2026, culminating in the grand final at MetLife Stadium, New York.
